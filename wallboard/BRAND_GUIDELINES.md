# C-Sema Tanzania: Brand Toolkit & UI Style Guide

This style guide defines the visual language for the **C-Sema Tanzania** child protection and helpline mobile application. The design focuses on fostering **trust, safety, and accessibility**, ensuring children and caregivers feel supported when seeking help.

---

## 1. Brand Identity & Colors

### Primary Palette
- **Deep Blue:** `#1D3E8A` (Headers, branding, trust)
- **Vibrant Orange:** `#F47C20` (Urgent actions, "Help" triggers)
- **Clean White:** `#FFFFFF` (Backgrounds, cards)

### Supporting Palette
- **Highlight Yellow:** `#FFD54F` (Accents, labels)
- **Light Blue:** `#E8F0FA` (Section backgrounds, secondary buttons)
- **Soft Gray:** `#F2F2F2` (Dividers, input backgrounds)
- **Dark Gray:** `#4A4A4A` (Body text)

---

### 3. Logo & Iconography
*   **Official Logos:** The application uses the original C-Sema Tanzania logo alongside the National Child Helpline 116 branding.
*   **Logo Treatment:** Logos are housed in a white rounded container (`--border-radius-md`) to ensure visibility and brand integrity across both light and dark themes.
*   **Icon Style:** Line-based, high-stroke weight for visibility on low-density displays.
*   **Scale:** Logos scale dynamically from 38px (monitor) up to 70px (4K TV).

---

## 4. Typography Hierarchy
- **Headings:** Deep Blue (#1D3E8A), Bold, Rounded Sans-Serif.
- **Body Text:** Dark Gray (#4A4A4A), Regular.

---

## 5. UI Components
- **Buttons:** Large, rounded corners (12px+). Vibrant Orange for primary actions.
- **Cards:** White background, soft shadows, rounded corners.
- **Icons:** Friendly, rounded, approachable.

---

1. **Safety First:** Balanced use of blue (calm) and orange (energy).
2. **Accessibility:** Large touch targets and high contrast.
3. **Approachable:** Friendly visuals and rounded elements.
